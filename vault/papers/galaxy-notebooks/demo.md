# Demo Plan

## Goal

Show that the notebook is not an after-the-fact note. It is the authored communication surface from which reusable workflow structure and report content can be recovered.

## Narrative Beats

1. Start with a real Galaxy history and a polished notebook.
2. The notebook references the outputs that matter.
3. Trigger workflow extraction from the notebook.
4. Galaxy walks backward through the history graph from referenced artifacts.
5. User confirms or prunes the graph.
6. Galaxy creates a workflow and carries the notebook narrative into the workflow report.

## Success Criteria

- The audience sees why a graph confirmation surface is better than a flat job list.
- The report continuity is visible.
- Chat is visibly optional rather than the headline.
